About Me

Prakash Rajan is the author of the short story collection 10½ Stories of Our Times (2017) and the novel Stories They Won’t Tell (2026). An engineer by training and an MBA graduate of the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign, he works on the business side of healthcare technology and consulting. A lifelong cinephile and an avid reader of literary fiction, he lives in the northwest suburbs of Chicago.
